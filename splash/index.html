Saaral Naadaga Kuzhu(SNK) is a Chennai based theatre company, well versed in various art forms of theatre like ‘Medai Naadagam’ (Stage play), ‘Theru Koothu’ (Street play), ‘Vasana Vaasipu’ (Script reading) etc., Essentially, a group of teenagers formed a platform to let loose their imagination in a field where they had zero experience but their steadfast imagination and determination has pushed this college based group into a professional one.

We do shows all over Chennai and with perseverance and struggle, have crossed some milestones along the way. Not only SNK but each of its members have their own personal achievements to boast of. Being a young group is not easy but we have had an exponential growth in terms of success. So, don’t forget to catch our next show to see this promising new theatre company rock the stage!
